SUPERMAN X GAS Collection

LOOK UP, IN THE SKY

GAS proudly launches an exclusive capsule in collaboration with Superman, celebrating the long-awaited return of the legendary Superhero with the highly anticipated theatrical release on July 11, 2025 — a full decade in the making.

The SUPERMAN X GAS capsule takes a darker, elevated approach with sleek black and white base tones, offering a refined, evening wear edge that speaks to both fashion-conscious customers and comic book lovers. Grounding neutrals form the perfect canvas for striking pops of Superman’s signature red and blue, bold metallic prints echo the strength and brilliance of Superman’s iconic Shield, keeping the heart of the Superhero alive in every piece.

Iconic DC comic artworks reimagined through a contemporary lens—offbeat placements, merging graphic nostalgia with street-ready sophistication. Offering a range with a perfect mix of casual yet edgy silhouettes comprising classic tees and polos with interesting logo placements, and boxy tees with minimal graphic language. Key pieces vary from Sweats with all over tonal flock print in classic black on black sophisticated take, to bold back print oversized edgy pieces. Designed for those who grew up idolizing the Superhero, as well as a new generation of comic book lovers discovering his legacy, this collection bridges timeless heroism with modern-day style.
