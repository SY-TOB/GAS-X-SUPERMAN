GAS X SUPERMAN Collection

LOOK UP, IN THE SKY

GAS proudly launches an exclusive capsule in collaboration with Superman, celebrating the long-awaited return of the legendary hero with the highly anticipated film release on July 11, 2025 — a full decade in the making

This big screen reboot directed by James Gunn introduces a more grounded, emotionally layered Clark Kent — a hero caught between two worlds, fighting to find his place while continuing to stand as a symbol of hope

With a mix of high-stakes action and deeper storytelling, the film redefines what it means to be Superman for today's world.

Superman, the Man of Steel, is one of the most iconic superheroes ever created. Born Kal-El on the planet Krypton, he was sent to Earth as a baby and raised as Clark Kent. With his incredible powers and unwavering moral compass, Superman has been a symbol of hope and justice for generations. His famous motto "Truth, Justice, and the American Way" has inspired millions worldwide.

GAS X SUPERMAN

The GAS X SUPERMAN capsule takes a darker, elevated approach with sleek black and white base tones, offering a refined, evening wear edge that speaks to both fashion-conscious customers and comic book lovers. Grounding neutrals form the perfect canvas for striking pops of Superman’s signature red and blue, bold metallic prints echo the strength and brilliance of Superman’s iconic armour, keeping the heart of the hero alive in every piece. Iconic DC comic artworks reimagined through a contemporary lens—offbeat placements, merging graphic nostalgia with street-ready sophistication. Designed for those who grew up idolizing the Man of Steel, as well as a new generation of comic book lovers discovering his legacy, this collection bridges timeless heroism with modern-day style.
